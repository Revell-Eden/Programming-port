This portfolio website marked the beginning of my malleable journey into the world of a Front-End Website developer. In commencing this project i quickly learned how the different functions of a development team can become extensive if not properly organised and assigned. Though I spent the dawning hours of the project cultivating the content of this page, I found myself rewriting aspects. I feel the experience of building something new blobk by block resonates with a quote by Georg C.Lichtenburg;

"I cannot say whether things will get better if we change; what I can say is they must change if they are to get better."

As my skills diversified, I developed a taste for intricacy--the minor details moulding my vision of a desirable portfolio.

This Website displays a plethora of;
-Dynamic Animations
-Interactive Navigable menu
-Glassmorphic Content Panels allowing a light weight design
-Live Statistic Trackers
